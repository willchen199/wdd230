NP Membership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee.

Bronze Membership (15% discounts on events, 10% discount on advertising, Fee of $50/month)

Silver Membership (25% discount on events, 15% discount on advertising, Free participation in the trade fair, Fee of $100/month)

Gold Membership (50% discount on events, 50% discount on advertising, Free participation in the trade fair, 5 free featured ads per year, 1 free training per quarter, Fee of $100/month)

This memberships have increasing costs as well as increased benefits per level like trainings, advertising (like on the home page spotlights), event discounts, etc.
